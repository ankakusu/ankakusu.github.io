Laser focus on your goal

01 Jun 2020 - by Yaprak

I often see people start with a topic but get distracted. Human are curious by nature, and the more you focus, the more you discover new things. Learning is endless and if you jump to another before you finish one, you will end up too many loose ends. This is overwhelming. Define your goal for a certain topic. Keep a laser focus on that. If another idea pops up, add that to your todo list and then keep working until you finish it. Finishing a task makes you feel happy! Now, you are ready to clear out your todo list.
